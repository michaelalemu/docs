## Welcome to FXEthio

Welcome to FXEthio, the premier platform for tracking and managing Foreign Exchange (FX) rates for Ethiopian Birr (ETB) from various Ethiopian banks, against major global currencies. With FXEthio you can easily access real-time exchange rate data for ETB in relation to currencies like USD, EUR, GBP, and more.
